# Reflection: Industry Talk 1 (PPG)
**Date:** 20 November 2025
**Venue:** Hyflex Classroom N28A

### 1. What is PPG?
PPG is a global supplier of paints, coatings, and specialty materials. This talk focused on their IT infrastructure and how they utilize technology to manage global operations.

### 2. Key Learnings
The talk featured several speakers who introduced different IT domains:
* **Data Analytics:** Derrick Tan (Data Analytics Manager) explained how data is used to drive business decisions.
* **Cloud Architecture:** Vijaykumar Dhandapani discussed the "Global Infrastructure" that supports PPG's worldwide connectivity.
* **SAP Operations:** Patrick Thong introduced SAP (Systems, Applications, and Products), showing how enterprise software manages business processes.

### 3. Impact on My Goal
This talk opened my eyes to the scale of IT in multinational companies. I realized that Computer Science is not just about coding, but also about managing massive infrastructures (Cloud) and analyzing data to improve efficiency. It motivated me to explore **Data Analytics** as a potential career path.
