# Reflection: UTM Digital Open Day
**Date:** 28 October 2025
**Location:** Dewan Sultan Iskandar, UTM

### 1. Activity Overview
I attended the UTM Digital Open Day, an event designed to showcase the latest digital innovations and technological advancements within the university. The event featured various booths from faculty departments and industry partners.

### 2. Key Learnings
* **AI Implementation:** I learned about the future roadmap for integrating Artificial Intelligence into UTM's digital ecosystem to improve student services.
* **Campus Innovations:** I explored booths displaying student projects and research, which gave me a broader perspective on how technology is applied in real-world scenarios.
* **Networking:** It was a great opportunity to interact with seniors and lecturers outside of the classroom environment.

### 3. Personal Reflection
This event opened my eyes to the vibrant tech culture at UTM.
* **Impact:** It motivated me to look beyond my current syllabus and stay updated with emerging trends like AI and Data Analytics.
* **Goal:** I hope to one day exhibit my own final year project at an event like this.
